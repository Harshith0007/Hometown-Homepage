Top three activities to do in Bangalore

Beat the heat at Wonderla

Wonderla Amusement Park is the best place to beat the summer heat and quench your thirst for some thrilling experience.

Go crazy for the night

Bangalore is known for it's party lifestyle. You will easily find the right club for you.

Go shopping at flea markets

Mg Road is known for it's variety of shops ranging from branded shops to first copy branded shops. Find the right choice for you, and bargain till you find it to be worth the price.
